[About]

Secure. Robust. Verifiable.

The Validator Operations Standard (ValOS) sets the benchmark for running staking infrastructure with confidence. Built on proven governance and risk frameworks, ValOS provides node operators with a comprehensive toolkit to identify and mitigate risks, improve internal processes, strengthen infrastructure, and ensure operational excellence.

ValOS originated from the DUCK initiative and was developed as a joint effort between industry leaders across staking, risk, security, and infrastructure operations. Contributors include:

Together, this collective ensures that ValOS is not just a theoretical standard—but an industry-DRIVEN benchmark for secure and resilient validator operations.
